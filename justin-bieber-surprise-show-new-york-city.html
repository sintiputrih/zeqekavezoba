Justin Bieber Gave a Surprise Performance at a New York City Nightclub

New York City residents who decided to have a quiet Friday night in, prepare for some serious FOMO. Last night into early this morning Justin Bieber gave an impromptu performance at Up and Down nightclub (where one of the many Met Gala after-parties were held just earlier this week). The singer was in town for his sold out Purpose World Tour show in Brooklyn and his pop up shop at VFiles.

Showing off his recently buzzed head and a baggy t-shirt and shorts, the Biebs sang "What Do You Mean" while casually sitting on top of the DJ stand as if this was no big deal. As you can tell by the many cell phones up in the air and the packed scene, it clearly was.
