Adria Arjona Wiki/Biography

Born on April 25 1992, Adria Arjona’s age is 30 Years Old as of 2023. Arjona was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico. She was a daughter of Leslie Torres, her mother and a Puerto Rican, and Ricardo Arjona, her father, who is a Guatemalan singer and songwriter. Arjona lived in Mexico City until she was 12 years old.

Arjona got to travel often during her childhood with her father, who took her along on his tours. In a discussion with her “Morbius” co-star Jared Leto for Interview Magazine, Adria has been a frequent visitor of Ricardo’s concerts, especially to those that took place in and around Latin America.

On tours, as she could recollect in one of her discussions with Jared Leto, who was cast alongside her as a costar in the movie Morbius, she helped her father sell the tour merchandise. She had also disclosed in the same interview for Interview Magazine that she lived on tour buses much of her life due to the busy schedule that her father used to have those days when she was with him.

She went to Miami when she was 12 years of age and stayed there until she turned 18. After that, she decided to move out to New York City on her own.

She joined Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute where she chose to study acting and while she was enrolled in the institution she worked as a waitress and a hostess. She has completed her School and College at San Juan University.
