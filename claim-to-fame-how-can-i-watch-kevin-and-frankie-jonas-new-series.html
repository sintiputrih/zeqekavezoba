Claim to Fame: How can I watch Kevin and Frankie Jonas' new series?

THE Jonas Brothers are widely known as a trio of Nick, Joe, and Kevin, but there is also another Jonas sibling.

Frankie Jonas is teaming up with his big brother Kevin Jonas for a new TV series.

How can I watch Claim to Fame?

The youngest Jonas brother, Frankie, 21, is stepping up as the host of the new series Claim to Fame.

PEOPLE magazine revealed that the show was greenlit by ABC and is being produced by Kinetic Content.

The production company is also behind the popular reality shows Love is Blind and Married at First Sight.

Claim to Fame is a series that will see 12 celebrity relatives be challenged by having to “step outside their famous family member’s shadow and live together under one roof, concealing their identity and lineage in the quest for their own fame and fortune.”

Those competing on the TV series will be able to win $100,000 in grand prize money and their own “claim to fame.”

According to the outlet, the celebrity relatives will compete in challenges, form alliances in the house, and “play DNA detective.”

The show airs on Monday, July 11, at 10pm EST on ABC.

Viewers can watch the show on ABC or on the app. Folks can also stream the series on Hulu.

Who is Frankie Jonas?

Frankie is the youngest sibling of all the Jonas Brothers.

He was born on September 28, 2000, making him 21 years old.

His other brothers are Kevin, 34, Joe, 32, and Nick, 29.

On March 23, Kevin took to Twitter to announce the news of the show.

“Exciting news!! @FrankieJonas and I will be co-hosting the new @ABCNetwork series #ClaimToFame Can’t wait for you all to see @ClaimToFameABC!!”

When does Claim to Fame premiere?

Claim to Fame will premiere on ABC on Monday, July 11.

In an interview with Bustle in 2021, Frankie spoke with the outlet about being called the “bonus Jonas” for years.

“I always hated that nickname,” he said.

“A lot of my problem with being in the public eye was that I was seen as this commodified vision of myself.”

Frankie has found his own fame outside the world of his three older brothers.

He is now one of the most popular stars on the video-sharing app TikTok.

“This experience on TikTok is so different because I’m being so authentically myself and not caring and just doing what I can.”
