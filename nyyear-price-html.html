Nyyear Price - Age, Family, Bio

Social media personality who posts humorous relationship videos, including pranks and skits, for more than 1.1 million followers on Instagram. He has also posted rap music videos on his YouTube channel. 

Before Fame

He grew up in Kentucky. 

Trivia

He was one half of the YouTube couples channel Nyyear AndJalyn, where the pair posted pranks, challenges and reactions for over 2.6 million subscribers. He has posted multiple pictures of himself standing in front of luxury cars wearing bags from brands like Louis Vuitton. 

Family Life

He is from Cincinnati, Ohio. He has dated Jalyn Michelle, the co-star of Nyyear AndJalyn. 

Associated With

He posted a reaction video to the 6IX9INE, Fetty Wap and A Boogie wit da Hoodie song "KEKE" in January 2018.
