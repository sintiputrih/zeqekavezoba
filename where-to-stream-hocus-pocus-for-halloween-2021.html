Where to Stream Hocus Pocus for Halloween 2021

October is finally here, and what better way to celebrate than by cozying up with a spooky movie? One of the most popular Halloween films out there is 1993’s Hocus Pocus, which stars Sarah Jessica Parker, Bette Midler, and Kathy Najimy as three witch sisters whose plot to become immortal is disrupted by a trio of local kids.

The movie is so beloved that it’s getting a long-awaited Disney+ sequel in 2022, with all three stars set to reprise their roles. So until Hocus Pocus 2 premieres, here’s everything you need to know about streaming Hocus Pocus:

IS HOCUS POCUS ON NETFLIX?

No, sorry. You won’t find Hocus Pocus on Netflix.

IS HOCUS POCUS ON FREEFORM? HOW TO WATCH HOCUS POCUS:

Yes, Hocus Pocus is airing on Freeform this month as part of its “31 Nights of Halloween” schedule. Here’s when you can catch it on the network:

  • Thursday, Oct. 14 at 8:50 p.m. ET
  • Saturday, Oct. 16 at 7:15 p.m. ET
  • Sunday, Oct. 17 at 5:10 p.m. ET
  • Saturday, Oct. 23 at 6:05 p.m. ET
  • Sunday, Oct. 24 at 8:45 p.m. ET
  • Thursday, Oct. 28 at 9 p.m. ET
  • Saturday, Oct. 30 at 8:50 p.m. ET
  • Sunday, Oct. 31 at 9:20 p.m. ET

IS HOCUS POCUS ON DISNEY+?

Yep! Hocus Pocus is available to stream on Disney+, so if you have an active subscription to the streaming service, you can watch the spooky classic as many times as you’d like.

WHERE ELSE CAN I STREAM HOCUS POCUS?

If you’d like to rent or buy Hocus Pocus, you can find it on digital streaming services like Apple TV, Google Play, Prime Video, and Vudu.
