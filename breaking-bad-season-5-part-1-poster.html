Breaking Bad Season 5: Part 1 Poster

Recommended Videos

Though hit shows like Mad Men and The Killing are coming to an end, AMC fans have nothing to worry about as the best is still yet to come. Breaking Bad will enter its fifth season on July 15th and today we have the first promo poster.

Season four left us with a ton of questions and the finale was nothing short of explosive. Fans have been waiting with baited breath to see where things would pick up and if this first poster is any indication, it looks like Walt is about take over the “throne.”

Aaron Paul, who plays Jesse on the show, recently said via Twitter that: “Breaking Bad will return to your Television sometime in July. Make sure u wear your diapers because I’m pretty sure u will shit yourself.” [sic]

Sounds like we’re in for one hell of a season.
