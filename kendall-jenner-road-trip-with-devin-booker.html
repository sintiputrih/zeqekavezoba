Kendall Jenner Road-Trips to Sedona with NBA Star Devin Booker

  • Kendall Jenner and NBA star Devin Booker went on a quick road trip to Sedona, Arizona, together, seen in new video obtained by TMZ.

Kendall Jenner might have a new man in her life.

In video obtained by TMZ, the fashion model was spotted in a Sedona, Arizona, parking lot with Phoenix Suns basketball player Devin Booker. In the clip, the two are seen getting into Booker's super-pricey Mercedes-Benz Maybach, with Jenner donning a white cropped tank top, khaki pants, and an olive-hued baseball cap.

"Kendall and friends have a small social circle amongst the group, who have been following the same social distancing and physical distancing guidelines. Devin is a friend and is part of the small group," a source told the outlet. "They took a road trip for some much-needed air."

The pair reportedly made a conscious effort to minimize contact with other people.

According to the outlet, Jenner and Booker have been friends for years and even double-dated at one point: when Jenner was with her on-and-off beau, Ben Simmons, and Booker was dating Kylie Jenner's former best friend, Jordyn Woods.

Booker is one of the top new players in the NBA and is currently under a five-year, $158 million contract with the Phoenix Suns.
