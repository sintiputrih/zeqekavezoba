How does Yanma learn ancient power?

Although it moves quickly, any type of Poké Ball in Pokémon Legends: Arceus can be used to catch Yanma. After Yanma has been found, players will need to raise it to level 34 so that Yanma can learn Ancient Power. Once a Yanma knows Ancient Power, it has everything it needs to evolve into Yanmega.

What level does Yanma learn ancient power?

Fortunately, players don't need to do much to get Ancient Power. Yanma will learn Ancient Power at level 33. Once the move is learned, players just need to level up one more time and it will evolve into Yanmega.

How do you teach Yanma ancient power arceus?

Yanma will learn the Ancient Power move when it reaches Level 33. Simply add the Ancient Power move on the list of its abilities and it will evolve into a Yanmega.
