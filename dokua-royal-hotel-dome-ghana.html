Dokua Royal Hotel, Dome, Ghana

Dokua Royal Hotel is situated in Accra,Ghana. Located 23 km from Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park. Dokua Royal Hotel features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking and a bar. They also havea 24-hour front desk and room service, along with access to free WiFi throughout the hotel. The hotel has family rooms.
The hotel serves continental breakfast each morning. Dokua Royal Hotel has a total of 17 rooms in 3 categories; all of the rooms come standard with AC, on demand hot water, TV, chair, and writing desk.
