Professional Career

She has achieved the great titles of Mixed Martial Artist(MMA), UFC, and Women’s Strawweight Division championship. After that, she made her professional MMA debut at Invicta FC: Esparza Vs Hyatt against Emily Kagan on January 5, 2013.

Through her victory in the first match, she faced another competition at Invicta FC 5: Penne vs Waterson against Kathina Catron on April 5, 2013. Following the double victory, she competes against Tecia Torres at Invicta FC6: Coenen Vs Cyborg. Unfortunately, she lost the match but she was announced that she was signed by Ultimate Fighting Championship(UFC) along with other 10 strawweight fighters to compete in 20 seasons of The Ultimate Fighter.
