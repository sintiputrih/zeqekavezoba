Dolphins vs. Jets live stream: watch the NFL on Black Friday

Black Friday is not only for shopping in 2023. For the first time, the NFL will air a live game on Black Friday. The game will feature an AFC East showdown between the Miami Dolphins (7-3) and the New York Jets (4-6) from MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey. Kickoff is scheduled for 3 p.m. ET/12 p.m. PT on November 24. Amazon will broadcast the game on Prime Video, with Al Michaels (play-by-play), Kirk Herbstreit (analyst), and Kaylee Hartung (reporter) on the call.

Prime Video memberships are available for $9/month, or you can subscribe to Amazon Prime for $15/month ($139/year) to receive shipping benefits for products bought through Amazon. For the Black Friday game, Amazon is offering a live stream of the game for free, even if you’re not a Prime Video subscriber or Amazon Prime member. You can sign up for a free Amazon account and stream the game. Additionally, Prime Video’s Twitch channel will simulcast the game.

Accessing the game on Prime Video is not the only way to stream Dolphins versus Jets. Fans in the New York and Miami markets can watch the game on over-the-air TV stations. New York will air the game on the local Fox affiliate, while Miami residents can see the game on the local CBS affiliate. Fox is available on streaming television services, such as Sling TV.
