R-Truth Knocks CM Punk Out Of Top Spot In WWE Merchandise Sales

Move out of the way, CM Punk. R-Truth is on top of WWEShop to kick off 2024.

CM Punk has dominated WWE merchandise sales since returning to the company at Survivor Series. But a little over a month later, he’s been surpassed by none other than R-Truth.

Following Monday’s hilarious R-Truth vignette, which explained why he believes he’s a member of The Judgment Day, the company released two new T-shirts. You can check them out in the embedded tweet below.

It’s evident this storyline is connecting with the WWE Universe as both of Truth’s shirts have skyrocketed straight to the top of the best-selling merchandise on WWEShop, knocking CM Punk off in the process.

With The Judgment Day doing so well now in merchandise sales thanks to R-Truth, perhaps the group may want to reconsider making him a full-fledged member of the faction.
