Annabelle Mullican Dies in Traffic Collision Accident

No arrests have been made after a 10-year-old girl was killed in a car crash in Mountain Brook Saturday.

The Mountain Brook Police Department said the crash happened near the Mountain Brook Club.

The girl, identified by the Jefferson County Coroner as Annabelle Mullican, was taken to the hospital but was later pronounced dead.

Police said an officer saw a white SUV traveling at a high rate of speed on Old Leeds Road. After the SUV turned onto Cherokee Road, the officer turned on their emergency lights and siren in their marked patrol vehicle in an attempt to conduct a traffic stop.
