Halle Berry Spotted in Rare Sighting with Boyfriend Van Hunt During Trip to Orlando

Halle Berry is rarely spotted out in public with her boyfriend Van Hunt, but we have some new photos from a trip they took last week!

The couple traveled from Los Angeles to Orlando on Wednesday (May 5) and they were seen arriving at the airport in Florida that afternoon. Two days later, the couple was seen arriving at the airport for their flight back west.

Halle and Van, who is a singer-songwriter, just made their red carpet debut a couple weeks ago while attending the Oscars together.

Over the weekend, Halle put up a touching note for Mother’s Day.

“I’ll never get over the wondrous miracle of growing a life inside my body as I’ve never felt more myself than when I was carrying my two children. Motherhood is a real privilege. Happy Mother’s Day to all the mothers of the world! ?,” she said on Instagram.
