How to pronounce quatar

Is Qatar pronounced or cutter?

It is generally acceptable to say pronounce Qatar as kuh-TAR or cutter. The most authentic version of pronouncing Qatar in Arabic is probably closer to kuh-ter (with the right emphasis/inflection)

Why is Qatar pronounced cutter?

As /u/Nickolaus pointed out, “Cutter” and “Gutter” are closest to how Qataris themselves pronounce it. Also “Cuttar”, slight emphasis on the first syllable. The “ka-TAR” pronunciation was formerly more common in the English-speaking world.
