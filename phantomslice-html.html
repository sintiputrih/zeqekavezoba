Fanum - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber who is widely known for his JustFanum channel. He has garnered more than 850,000 subscribers there for his videos about New York life. One of his popular video segments featured him trying out different New York City deli foods. 

Before Fame

He began his Fanum YouTube channel on January 10, 2013.

Trivia

He surpassed 20,000 subscribers to his Fanum YouTube channel on May 23, 2016. One of his most popular YouTube videos, "New York Problems - Hood Edition," has earned more than 1 million views. His Instagram biography has listed him as an "introverted extrovert". 

Family Life

He was born and raised in New York. His first name is Roberto. He has a younger brother who's featured in his videos. He's of Dominican descent.

Associated With

He has collaborated with fellow YouTubers ShootEveryone, HankDaTank25 and ImDavisss.
