Cheyenne Frontier Days Rodeo: Air time, how to live stream, list of events and all you need to know

If you're a fan of all things country, then there some exciting news for you -- the Cheyenne Frontier Days has announced in 2021 night schedule. 2021 marks the 125th celebration of the annual rodeo festival, which celebrates all things western, and this year guarantees to be just as grand. CFD begins on July 23, all the way to August 1, 2021. 

The 'Big Daddy of 'Em All' is the world's largest outdoor rodeo festival, with pros competing against each other to win more $1 million in cash and prizes. Apart from the daily rodeo show, 'CFD' has a little something for everyone, from tours to wild house races, chuckwagon cook-offs to art shows and of course, the much-awaited musical performances featuring the best and biggest names in country music.

As reported by Billings Gazette, the festival, which is an annual celebration, was canceled last year, on account of the mounting Covid-19 cases. The cancelation resulted in a loss of a whopping $3.34 million. As per Tom Hirsig, CEO of Cheyenne Frontier Days, there will be no limits imposed on the crowds at events at the festival. However, organizers have taken into account necessary Covid-19 protocols to help maintain cleanliness and sanitation. 

Where to live stream the event?

In case you can't make it to CFD, you can always stay tuned with the action by catching the live stream of the same. While there is currently no information about where you can watch the same, check out the festival's official YouTube and Twitter page for updates. 

How to buy tickets

Tickets for the Cheyenne Frontier Days rodeo show can be purchased via the official website. Ticket sales go live on April 15, 2021. Click here for more information .

Similarly, tickets for the Frontier Nights, the concerts held at the festival also go live on April 15. You can also upgrade to the Party Zone Fast Pass to get in early and score a better spot. If you're planning to make an entire day out of it, the website also offers various packages that might suit your preference. 

What to expect?

Making up for its absence last year, CFD aims to be just as grand as ever, with its signature rodeo shows, such as bareback riding, barrel racing, bull riding, steer wrestling, tie-down rope and more. Cheyenne Frontier Days™ has a modified, tournament-style rodeo format. Every rodeo performance will see event money winners who will advance to semifinals and finals. Finals competition starts with a clean slate on Championship Sunday, where the highest scores and fastest times will be crowned champion!
