Who Is Bryan Redmond? Wiki / Biography

Bryan Redmond was born to mother Jill Marie and father Rick Redmond. He spent his childhood days in Dallas, Texas. His mother, a Southern Methodist University graduate, passed away in October 2020 in a car accident. Brinkley, his 9-year-old daughter, had survived a car accident that killed his mother Jill at the time.

Mr. Redmond grew up with his siblings – his sister Jill, and step-siblings Megan Hughey, Lauren Cranford, and Kristen Kosch. Bryan was a basketball player at Southwestern University in Texas. He earned his diploma in the year 2000. 

Bryan Redmond And Brandi Redmond Relationship Status 

In 2003, Bryan Redmond got married to high school sweetheart Brandi Redmond. In September 2020, the pair celebrated 17 years of marriage. The pair share four children – daughters named Brinkley, age 9, Brooklyn, age 11, and Brilynn, 2 months, and a son, Bruin, 2. Following a miscarriage, the couple adopted their only son, Bruin, in 2018. 

Prior to the video leak, there were rumors that Bryan had been unfaithful to Brandi. As rumors of infidelity emerged in February 2021, Brandi posted a cryptic message about preferring “happiness.”
