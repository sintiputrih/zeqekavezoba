Is gelato considered a custard?

Gelato. In Italian, the word “gelato” literally translates as “ice cream.” Even though gelato is made with a custard basis, unlike its American counterpart, it includes less milk fat and has less air stirred into it during the freezing process, which results in a thicker texture.
