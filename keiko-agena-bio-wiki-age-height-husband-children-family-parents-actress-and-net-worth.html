Keiko Agena Gilmore Girls

Keiko is popular for her role in Gilmore Girls, where she was cast as  Lane Kim, a Korean-American teenager, the best friend of Rory Gilmore, one of the main characters. She was 27 years and she managed to play the role despite being significantly older than her character, who was 16.

Keiko Agena Actress

As an actress, Agena has also played the role of Mearing’s Aide in Transformers: Dark of the Moon. She also appeared in three episodes of Felicity as Leila Foster, a girl who comes to the lead character for assistance in acquiring the morning-after pill.

She was a recipient of the Best Female Actor award in the Ammy Awards, which honor Asian and Asian-American achievement in film or television. She also provided the voice of Yori in the Disney cartoon series Kim Possible in seasons 2, 3, and 4 of the show. Agena plays a high school teacher in the Netflix series 13 Reasons Why.
