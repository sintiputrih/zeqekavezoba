Mark Rowley Bio (Wiki)

Who is Mark Rowley?

Mark Rowley is an actor of Scottish nationality. Mark Rowley is popular for featuring in Young Dracula (2014), Home Fires (2016), The North Water, etc.

Moreover, his acting in the TV series River City as Jamie McAllister is appreciated by the audience.

His upcoming screen projects The Eye and T.I.M. are in the post-production stage and they will release soon. As a producer plus writer he has worked in 2023’s short film Write Off.

Mark Rowley- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Mark Rowley’s full birthdate is 29 Jan 1990 and he was born in Paisley, Renfrewshire, Scotland. Similarly, the name of his father is Stephen Rowley and his mother’s identity is Ann Rowley.

Further, Mark Rowley’s ancestry is Scottish and as to his academics, he went to the PACE Theatre Company to study acting.
