Do you need a passport to leave Vatican City?

Yes, you need a passport to leave Vatican City. Despite its small size and unique status as a sovereign city-state, Vatican City operates like any other country when it comes to immigration and travel regulations. If you are planning to leave Vatican City and enter another country, you will be required to present your passport at the border controls. This is because Vatican City, although an independent state, does not have its own airport or seaport, which means that you will need to pass through the neighboring country of Italy to reach your final destination.
