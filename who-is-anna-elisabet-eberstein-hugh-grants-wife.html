Who is Anna Elisabet Eberstein, Hugh Grants wife?

Hugh John Mungo Grant is an English actor. He established himself early in his career as a charming and vulnerable romantic leading man and has since transitioned into a more dramatic character actor. Among his accolades, he has received a British Academy Film Award, a Golden Globe Award, and an Honorary César.

Who is Anna Elisabet Eberstein, Hugh Grant’s wife?

Anna Elisabet Eberstein has been the wife of Hugh Grant since 2018. The couple have been together for about five years now and they are blessed with four children. They welcomed first a son named John Mungo, born in 2012, a daughter, born in 2015; and their third baby, born in March 2018.

Eberstein is Swedish and was raised in Sweden but lives in Grant’s native London. She is the daughter of Susanne Eberstein and Hans Eberstein her parents graduated from Sweden’s Uppsala University in 2003 with a master’s degree in economics and also studied at Lincoln Memorial University in Tennessee. She has a sibling named Christian Eberstein.

Eberstein worked as a freelance television producer, but she was previously a promotions producer for ESPN, where she produced video material for marketing, advertising, and on-air promos.

Eberstein is also an entrepreneur. In 2014, she co-founded a slipper-sock brand called Ace & Me with her cousin Pia Tamm Hagno.
