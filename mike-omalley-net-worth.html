Mike O’Malley: From Children’s Game Show Host to Successful Actor

Mike O’Malley, an American actor and writer, has a net worth of $15 million dollars. He began his career with a small role on “Law & Order” in the early 90s. O’Malley then went on to host two children’s game shows, “Get the Picture” and “Nickelodeon GUTS”.

From there, O’Malley appeared on various television series such as “Life with Roger” and the game show “Figure It Out”. He also acted in movies like “Deep Impact”, “Pushing Tin”, and “28 Days”.

O’Malley starred on the short-lived “The Mike O’Malley Show” and is most recognized for his recurring and co-starring roles on “Yes, Dear”, “My Name is Earl”, “Justified”, and “Glee”. He appeared in 33 episodes of “Glee”.

His biggest career success so far has been “Yes, Dear”. At the peak of his work on the show, O’Malley earned $300,000 per episode. He also appeared in the films “So Undercover” and “R.I.P.D.”
