What sport gives the most scholarships?

When it comes to earning a scholarship for college, one of the most common paths is through sports. While many sports offer athletic scholarships, some are more generous than others. So, what sport gives the most scholarships?

According to recent data, football offers the most scholarships out of any sport. This is not surprising considering the popularity and revenue generated by college football programs. In Division I football alone, there are over 85,000 scholarships available. However, it is important to note that these scholarships are divided among both full and partial scholarships, and there is a lot of competition for them.

Basketball is another sport that offers a significant number of scholarships. In Division I men's basketball, there are over 13,000 scholarships available, while in women's basketball, there are over 18,000. Like football, these scholarships are divided among both full and partial scholarships.

Baseball and softball are also popular sports for earning scholarships. In Division I baseball, there are over 11,000 scholarships available, while in softball, there are over 9,000. These sports also offer a mix of full and partial scholarships.

Soccer is another sport that offers a significant number of scholarships. In Division I men's soccer, there are over 4,000 scholarships available, while in women's soccer, there are over 9,000. These scholarships are also divided among both full and partial scholarships.

Track and field, wrestling, volleyball, golf, swimming and diving, and tennis are other sports that offer scholarships, but the number of scholarships available is lower compared to football, basketball, baseball, and soccer.
