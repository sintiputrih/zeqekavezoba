Tracee Ellis Ross, 50, serves 'body goals' in leopard bikini

Tracee Ellis Ross is showing off her wild side.

The “Black-ish” actress, 50, looked as ageless as ever in new bikini photos she posted on Instagram Monday.

Ross struck a series of playful poses in the leopard-print two-piece, adding a plunging cream-colored cover-up, brown sandals and oversized glasses in the last snap.

And the Pattern Beauty founder’s friends and fans couldn’t get enough of the pics.

“The abs are abbing,” commented writer Adriane Jamison, while a Ross fan account wrote, “BODY GOALS,” adding, “50 has never looked so good.”

Joked Jesse Tyler Ferguson, “I want to come back reincarnated as you.”

January Jones and Padma Lakshmi both dropped fire emojis, while singer Chika told Ross, “you’re the flyest person i know.”

Throughout the week, the “Girlfriends” icon has been sharing vacation snaps from a tropical locale, showing off swim looks ranging from a sunny yellow set to a blue floral bikini.

In an interview on “Today” earlier this year, Ross spoke about the joys of turning 50.

“I think I’m the sexiest I’ve ever been. I feel fantastic and I feel like the world is my oyster,” she said.

In 2020, the stylish star accepted the Fashion Icon Award at the 2020 People’s Choice Awards, clad in a white Alexander McQueen shirtdress accessorized with a leather harness and thigh-high boots.

Ross thanked her ever-fashionable mom, Diana Ross, during her acceptance speech, calling the diva’s closet “the best fashion playground of all time.”

It seems the actress’ own closet (or suitcase, in this case) is quite the playground as well.
