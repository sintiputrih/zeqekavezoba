Crazy fan theory: Is Breaking Bad actually a prequel to The Walking Dead?

Some viewers have suggested that Breaking Bad could be viewed as a prequel to The Walking Dead.

They're both intensely popular shows with their own distinct worlds, but some fans have a question: Are Breaking Bad and The Walking Dead linked?

Some viewers have suggested that Breaking Bad - the story of down-on-his-luck teacher Walter White who starts cooking meth to make ends meet - could be viewed as a prequel to The Walking Dead - the gory drama about humans trying to survive in a post-apocalyptic zombie world.

Funnily enough, Netflix - which didn't originally air either of the shows - has jumped on board the theory, releasing a video this week to add fuel to the fire.

The video posits that Walter White accidentally started the zombie epidemic that erupts in episode one of The Walking Dead with the meth he created.
