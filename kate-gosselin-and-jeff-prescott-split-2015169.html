Kate Gosselin, Millionaire Boyfriend Jeff Prescott Split: Report

Kate, minus one. Kate Gosselin’s millionaire boyfriend, Jeff Prescott, has ended their relationship after eight months together. According to Entertainment Tonight, the COO and co-founder of Dreamstime, a microstock photography agency, is now dating a 26-year-old blonde.

ET reports that the pair called it quits after fighting over Labor Day weekend, when Prescott allegedly backed out of filming Gosselin’s reality show, Kate Plus 8. The divorced dad of three was also reportedly MIA while traveling the world, where he met his new girlfriend, a business major who is fluent in five languages and developing her own cosmetics lines.
