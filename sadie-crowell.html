Sadie Crowell - Instagram, Age, Net Worth, Nationality, Facts

Table of Biography

Sadie Crowell is a popular American YouTube star and TikTok star. Sadie Crowell is mostly famous for her daily vlogs on her YouTube channel and also for her posts on her Instagram account.

Early Life and Education

Sadie Crowell was born on August 21, 2004, in Cumming, GA, United States. As of 2022, her age is 18 years old.

Her birth sign is Leo and she holds American nationality. She has a younger brother named Cooper and an older brother named Wyatt.

Her father’s name is Lester and her mother’s name is Kelley. She spent her childhood with her siblings.

Regarding her educational background, she went to West Forsyth High School.

Sadie Crowell – Social Media

As a social media sensation, she is very active on her social media accounts and updates her fans about her daily lifestyle. Her self-titled YouTube channel (@sadiecrowell) has 565k subscribers.

She has an Instagram account with the username @sadiecrowelll which has 215k followers and her Twitter account (@sadiecrowelll) has 9k followers. Her Facebook account username is Sadie Crowell.

Similarly, her TikTok account (@sadiecrowell) has 603k followers.

Career and Professional Life

Sadie created her own YouTube channel on April 5, 2008. Her contents are based on her daily activities and her family also supports her vlogs.

Even her parents and siblings make appearances in her several vlogs such as Drive With Us!, Our Last Day Before Lockdown, and others.

Her YouTube videos are also categorized as a drive with my difficulties and cooking cuts. She has been posting lots of videos which is liked by her viewers and subscribers.

She posted her first YouTube video on June 8, 2019. The video is titled ‘I CAN’T BELIEVE SHE DID THIS.’ Also, some of her other old videos are titled as ‘She wasn’t happy I said this…,’ ‘Drive with me and Kelley (she started crying) !!!,’ and ‘Announcing what’s wrong with my little brother….’ Her first recording to exceed 300,000 views was 2019’s TELLING MY MOM THE TRUTH! (Hot Topics with Kelley).

In August 2019, she recorded a vlog at a Khalid concert.
