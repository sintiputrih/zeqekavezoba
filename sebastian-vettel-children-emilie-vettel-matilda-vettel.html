Sebastian Vettel is a German racing driver who currently drives for Aston Martin in Formula One.

He has previously raced for BMW Sauber, Toro Rosso, Red Bull, and Ferrari.

Sebastian Vettel has four World Drivers’ Championship titles, all of which he won in a row between 2010 and 2013. Vettel is Formula One’s youngest World Champion.

Sebastian Vettel has the third most race wins (53) and podium finishes (122), as well as the fourth most pole positions (57).

Sebastian Vettel began his Formula One career in 2006 as a test driver for BMW Sauber, with a single race appearance in 2007.

Sebastian Vettel joined Ferrari in 2015 and became Mercedes’ and Lewis Hamilton’s closest competitor in two championship battles, finishing second in both years.
