Z-Ro Net Worth

Z-Ro is a rapper from Houston, TX, born on January 19, 1977. He has been recognized by the New York Times as one of the most underrated rappers, and released his debut album, Look What You Did to Me, in 1998. He has since gone on to release numerous albums and mixtapes, and is considered one of the most influential rappers in the Houston hip-hop scene.
