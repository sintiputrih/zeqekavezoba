Booker T Teases He’s ‘Rumble Ready’

Booker T is no stranger to surprises, sucka, especially during the Royal Rumble. “The Hardcore, OG Brother from 110th Street” made a memorable return to the squared during the 2011 rendition of the Rumble and with the big event just a few weeks away, Booker doesn’t have that memory out of his head. He shared an image of himself “clangin’ and bangin'” in the gym, including the hashtag that says “Rumble Ready.” It’s interesting to note that The Revival and Booker have recently been playing with the notion that Dawson & Wilder would like to face Harlem Heat in a tag match.

Sarah Logan Certain She’ll See Charlotte In The Royal Rumble

Sarah Logan faced off against Charlotte Flair on RAW and felt the agony of defeat at the hands of The Queen. That doesn’t deter her hopes and assumptions that she’ll come face to face with the future Hall of Famer during the women’s Royal Rumble match in two weeks.

Despite falling short against Charlotte Flair on Raw, Sarah Logan is full of confidence should the two come face-to-face in the Women’s Royal Rumble Match.
