Jason Jordan Wishes Kurt Angle A Happy Father’s Day

It took a little bit of time throughout the day, but Jason Jordan finally got around to wishing his Olympic hero poppa a Happy Father’s Day. Kurt Angle was the first to make it known that he had yet to receive a phone call from his newly founded son, but all was rectified when the WWE Hall Of Famer received a special message from JJ via Twitter.

Happy Father’s Day to all of the dads out there. Still waiting on a call from my son Jason. All of my other kids wished me a happy Father’s Day. Jason, you have until midnight, or mom is gonna ground you for a week. #itstrue

Kevin Owens, AJ Styles & Shelton Benjamin Join UUDD

Five WWE Superstars fill the room for Xavier Woods on one of the latest installments of the UpUpDownDown YouTube channel and it’s all for the sake of playing Scream Go Hero!

What’s louder than putting five WWE Superstars in one room? When those WWE Superstars happen to be AJ Styles (a.k.a. K.O.P), Shelton Benjamin (a.k.a VG OG), Ariya Daivari (a.k.a Big Div), Brian Kendrick (a.k.a. Fighting Thumbs) and Kevin Owens who have to scream and shout while playing Scream Go Hero!
