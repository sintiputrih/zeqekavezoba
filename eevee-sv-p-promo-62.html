Eevee (Japanese: イーブイ Eievui) is a Colorless-type Basic Pokémon card. It is one of the SV-P Promotional cards.

Card text

Continuous Dash
れんぞくダッシュ		20+
Flip a coin until you get tails. This attack does 20 more damage for each heads.

Pokédex data

Release information

In Japan, this card was released as a SV-P Promotional card and was given away as part of the YU NAGABA × Pokémon Card Game merchandise campaign. Customers could get a collaboration promo card for every ¥1,000 worth of eligible products purchased at Pokémon Centers, Pokémon Stores, or the Pokémon Center Online website in Japan, up to a limit of 10 cards. The cards were distributed at random. People who managed to pre-order a copy of the YU NAGABA × Pokémon Card Game Eeveelution Special Box, which was allocated by lottery on the Pokémon Center Online, also received 4 random promotional cards copy in their shipment.

The "YU NAGABA × Pokémon Card Game" campaign logo is featured in the bottom right corner of the card artwork.

Trivia

Origin

This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Scarlet.
