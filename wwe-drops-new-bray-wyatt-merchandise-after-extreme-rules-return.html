WWE Drops New Bray Wyatt Merchandise After Extreme Rules Return

WWE got the wrestling world talking with white rabbit QR code tease. The door was finally opened and Bray Wyatt arrived at the Wells Fargo Center to a roaring ovation on October 8th, Saturday. WWE is capitalizing on the return in a huge way.

WWE dropped new Bray Wyatt merchandise and fans can’t get them all through WWEShop.com. The company has released a Bray Wyatt moth T-shirt (available in both men and youth sizes) and a pullover hoodie (available in men’s size).

As previously reported, Wyatt’s return drew huge numbers for WWE’s social media platforms. Wyatt was already a top draw when he was released from his contract in 2021 and he is expected to retain his momentum in regards to his merchandise sales.

Ringside News exclusively reported that Bray Wyatt’s WWE return was in motion prior to Vince McMahon’s exit. In fact, it was pretty solid even before Triple H took control of creative.

Bray Wyatt has got the whole world in his hands once again. We’ll have to wait and see what WWE has in store for Wyatt not that he’s back. Keep reading Ringside News for more.
