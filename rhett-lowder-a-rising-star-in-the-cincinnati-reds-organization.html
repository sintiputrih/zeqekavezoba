Rhett Lowder: A Rising Star in the Cincinnati Reds Organization

Rhett Lowder, the talented right-handed pitcher from Wake Forest, made headlines when he was selected by the Cincinnati Reds with the seventh overall pick in the 2023 MLB draft. With his impressive college record, Lowder was expected to be a quick mover through the minors, and fans have been eagerly awaiting news of his progress. So, where is Rhett Lowder playing now?

After signing with the Reds in July 2023, Lowder was assigned to the team's Class A affiliate, the Dayton Dragons. He wasted no time in making an impact, striking out six batters in just two innings in his debut game. Since then, he has continued to impress, showcasing his mid-90s fastball and above-average slider in each outing.

Lowder's first full season in the minors has been a resounding success. He currently boasts a 10-2 record with a 2.15 ERA, striking out 125 batters in just 83 innings pitched. His performance has earned him recognition as one of the top prospects in the Reds' farm system.

Baseball experts have lauded Lowder's competitiveness, pitchability, and stuff, noting that he has the potential to be a major league starting pitcher. His ability to mix pitches and keep hitters off-balance has been a particular strength, and he has shown poise and maturity beyond his years on the mound.

Lowder's success on the field has not gone unnoticed by the Reds organization. In August 2024, he was promoted to the team's Double-A affiliate, the Chattanooga Lookouts. While the jump to Double-A can be a challenging one for young players, Lowder has continued to impress. In his first start with the Lookouts, he struck out eight batters over six innings, allowing just two hits and no runs.

Despite his success thus far, Lowder remains focused on improving his game. He has been working closely with the Reds coaching staff to refine his mechanics and develop his changeup into a plus pitch. His dedication and work ethic have drawn praise from his coaches and teammates alike.

Looking ahead, Lowder's trajectory seems to be pointing towards a future in the majors. While it's impossible to predict the exact timeline of his ascent, many believe that he could be in line for a call-up as early as the 2025 season.

Rhett Lowder is currently playing for the Chattanooga Lookouts, the Double-A affiliate of the Cincinnati Reds. Since being drafted in 2023, he has made a meteoric rise through the Reds' minor league system, earning recognition as one of the top prospects in the organization. With his impressive arsenal of pitches and competitive mindset, Lowder has the potential to be a star in the majors. Fans of the Reds will no doubt be eagerly anticipating his arrival at Great American Ball Park in the near future.
