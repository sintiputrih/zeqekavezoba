    StepsDescription

    2. Choose Stripe and Salesforce as your apps:Once you've logged in, choose Stripe and Salesforce as your apps from the list of available apps.
    3. Choose a trigger and an action:Next, choose a trigger and an action for your integration. For example, you can choose 'New Email' as the trigger for Stripe and 'Post a Message' as the action for Salesforce.
    4. Connect your accounts:After selecting the trigger and action, connect your Stripe and Salesforce accounts to Appy Pie Connect. Follow the on-screen instructions to enter your login credentials and authorize the connection.
