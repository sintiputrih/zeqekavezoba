Ami James Net Worth

Ami James is an Israeli-born entrepreneur and tattoo artist. He is the owner of Love Hate Tattoos, which was featured on TLC's show, Miami Ink. He is also the co-owner of the DeVille clothing company and Love Hate Lounge, a Miami nightclub. Additionally, he opened a London tattoo parlor called Love Hate Social Club. He was born on April 6, 1972.
