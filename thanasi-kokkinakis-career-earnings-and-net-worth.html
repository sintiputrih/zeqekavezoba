Thanasi Kokkinakis career earnings and net worth

Thanasi Kokkinakis, 27,  is an Australian professional tennis player. He has a career-high ATP singles ranking of world No. 69 achieved on 17 January 2022. He has won one ATP Tour title, the 2022 Australian Open men’s doubles title with Nick Kyrgios.

Kokkinakis is known for his powerful serve and his aggressive style of play. He is also known for his sense of humor and his outgoing personality. He is a popular figure in Australia and is seen as a rising star in the sport.

In 2022, Kokkinakis reached the final of the Adelaide International, where he lost to Aslan Karatsev. He then won the Australian Open men’s doubles title with Nick Kyrgios, defeating Rinky Hijikata and Jason Kubler in the final.

How much does Thanasi Kokkinakis make a year?
