Harbinger D20 | Muzzle in Warzone, MW3 and MW2

The Harbinger D20 is a weapon attachment featured in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 (2023), Modern Warfare 2 (2022), and Call of Duty: Warzone.

It is one of the Muzzle attachments available in MW3 and MW2.

The Harbinger D20 is a Universal Attachment, which means it can be used across all Weapons in the game.
