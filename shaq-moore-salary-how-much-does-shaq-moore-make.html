Shaq Moore Salary: How much does Shaq Moore make?

Shaq Moore currently earns $13,462 gross per week and $700,000 per year. He hails from Powder Springs, Georgia, in the United States of America.

Shaq Moore made his full international debut on June 2, as a 70th-minute substitute for DeAndre Yedlin in a 1–2 friendly loss away to the Republic of Ireland.

Shaq Moore was included in a first-team matchday for the first time on September 30, 2017, for a La Liga match against Deportivo Alavés.

Shaq Moore started his first game for Levante on November 18, 2017, in a 2–0 away defeat of UD Las Palmas. Moore became only the fourth American to play in La Liga after Kasey Keller, Jozy Altidore, and Oguchi Onyewu.

On July 30, 2022, Shaq Moore made his debut for Major League Soccer side Nashville SC 11 days later in a 1–1 home draw with Vancouver Whitecaps FC, playing for 63 minutes.

Shaq Moore represented the United States at the under-17, under-18, and under-20 levels. Shaq captained the under-17s in 2012–13, including at the 2013 CONCACAF U-17 Championship.
