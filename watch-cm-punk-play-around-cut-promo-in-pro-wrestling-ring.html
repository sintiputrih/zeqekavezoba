Watch CM Punk Play Around &amp; Cut Promo In Pro Wrestling Ring

CM Punk will be at Starrcast this Saturday before All Out. Needless to say, it’s going to be a very busy day.

The Second City Savior recently took the time to cut a little promo for Starrcast and he had a lot of fun in the process. It was also evident that he threw a reference in there to Aleister Black and Zelina Vega by posing like them.

He didn’t know what to say and then he took a roll which is the closest to a pro wrestling move that we’re likely to see from him. “That’s a bump, $20,000, thanks, Conrad,” CM Punk said.

In all seriousness, CM Punk did cut a promo and he made a point to say that he’s not canceling, because “that’s what everybody does at Starrcast.” He said his mouth has gotten him in trouble before, so let’s see if it happens again this Saturday.
