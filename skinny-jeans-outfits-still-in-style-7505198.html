13 Skinny Jean Outfits That Will Never Go Out of Style

As part of the great fashion circle of life, denim trends come and go. What was once the height of fashion will one day become cheugy before surging back to the top of the TikTok trending video charts. One seemingly on-the-way-out fashion trend that stays both winning and decisive? Skinny jean outfits. Fortunately for millennials, we have the answer to the question: "Are skinny jeans still in style?" Yes, even if TikTok tells you otherwise.

Granted, today's best skinny jeans have evolved since the late aughts. For one, the skinny jean silhouette has softened. Originally low-rise, the waistline of skinny jeans has fluctuated over the years, too. Wear them low, high, or mid-rise â€” whatever you prefer. At the end of the day, the focal point of skinny jeans is a tapered ankle and a snug fit throughout.

If you're in need of a little inspo to give your most beloved pair of denim a refresh, check out our favorite skinny jean outfits below. 

Try a Stirrups Spin-Off

You've heard of stirrup leggings, but what about stirrup skinny jeans? These VB-approved bottoms pair perfectly with a knit crop top and matching shrug.

Say "Yes" to Denim on Denim

Canadian tuxedos don't have to leave you swimming in yards of denim. Give denim on denim an update by pairing embellished skinny jeans with a matching denim top. Leave a few unbuttons undone for that street-style star touch.

Channel the Girl Next Door

If jeans and a T-shirt are your go-to outfit formula, you're bound to love this slight variation. Sub in your tee for a crop top, and throw in some strappy sandals while you're at it. It's simple yet sexy, making it the quintessential girl-next-door look.

Break Out a White Pair

A slinky metallic blouse is practically begging to be paired with white skinny jeans. Strappy sandals are the finishing touch to this going-out outfit, but white sneakers will work just as well.

Go Slightly Shorter

Draw attention to a pair of chic loafers or flats with cropped skinny jeans that hit at the ankle. This look works well with minimal, quiet luxury staples but is also a great backdrop to show off a statement jacket like Olivia Palermo does here.

Embrace Two Tones

One clever way to update a skinny jeans outfit is to skip a single shade and embrace a two-toned or dual-textured pair. Much like brightly colored jeans add flair to a 'fit, leather-trimmed skinny jeans instantly elevate an OOTD.

Finish with Heels

Perhaps the most celeb-y way to wear skinny jeans is with a sleek heel. The look is a classic for a reason and infinitely customizable depending on your personal sense of style.

Throw on a Blazer

Light-wash skinnies lean casual but become instantly elevated when paired with a blazer. Finish with flats, heels, or even sandals, depending on the exact formal-to-informal ratio you're going for.

Load Up on Layers

There's nothing more effortless and edgy than a pair of skinny jeans worn with ankle boots, as demonstrated here. Layer up with statement outerwear, sweaters, and the like for the ultimate wintry ensemble.

Strike a Balance

Balance casual and chic by pairing denim flats and a sleek trench coat. The silhouette of the trench is pure high-end fashion, while flats in a shade of denim that matches your jeans add a touch of street-style minimalism.

Reach for a Statement Top

Skinny jeans are truly the "less is more" jean style. They create a subtle, muted backdrop for whatever else you're wearing (see: Tayshia Adams's ruffle top), which makes them the perfect pants to pair with a major statement piece.

Play with Proportions

Hop on the jellyfish trend by complementing your skinnies with a bulky top, such as an oversized blazer, puffer coat, chunky sweater, or voluminous confection of choice. Platform sneakers are the perfect add-on.

Add a Western Twist

Calling all coastal cowgirls: Do your favorite cowboy boots a favor and wear them with a pair of skinny jeans. Combining a new trend with a wardrobe staple will give the latter an instant refresh.
